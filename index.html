About NeuroTouch

NeuroTouch is a high-fidelity virtual reality simulator designed to assist in the training of neurosurgeons. It offers a physics-based simulation environment that mimics cranial microneurosurgery scenarios using real-time haptic feedback, visual fidelity, and tissue interaction. The platform tracks a range of performance metrics and enables comparison between novice and expert users, contributing to surgical education and assessment in a controlled environment.

Vision Behind the Simulation

National Research Council Canada

Lead Organization

"Advancing surgical training with realism, precision, and science."

Learn More

The NeuroTouch project was spearheaded by NRC Canada with input from neurosurgeons, computer scientists, and medical educators. The system enables the simulation of key surgical actions—such as tumor removal—and records detailed cognitive and biomechanical data. This initiative represents a leap forward in using immersive technology to improve medical outcomes through better training.

Shaping the Future of Surgical Education

NeuroTouch has set a foundation for the development of future virtual simulators in the medical field. It emphasizes realism and data-driven evaluation, providing a safe yet effective environment to learn high-stakes surgical skills. With the continued integration of AI and machine learning, simulators like NeuroTouch are paving the way toward personalized surgical training and certification systems.
